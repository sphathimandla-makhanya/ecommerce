About us

Food on the go is a South African fast food outlet that was founded by Life Choices in 2023 and sponsored by Pioneer foods.

Our Mission

  • To be the best fast food outlet in the region.
  • To serve fresh food to our customers
  • To be the first choice food destination

Our Vision

  • To have an outlet in every city, town and village in the country
  • To bring out smiles and joy in the heart of our customers
  • To eradicate hunger
